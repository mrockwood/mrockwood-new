Working on this site was a great learning experience for my team. It featured an early attempt at component-based design, and a primitive style guide—processes we would refine in later projects.

My role was primarily working on the front-end. It was built using Foundation, jQuery, and Slick Slider.

One of our biggest challenges was designing and implementing a responsive content slider featuring news aggregated from various content hubs within the organization.
