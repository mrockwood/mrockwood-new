Visit Website

This project was initiated when Vice President Joe Biden scheduled a visit to the Huntsman Cancer Institue (HCI) as part of his “moonshot” cure for cancer. Expecting a surge in interest, HCI wanted a modern, usable website ready for new visitors. With a hard deadline and many interested parties involved, I was able to transform designer Drew Corkill's work into a clean, friendly, content-driven web experience.
